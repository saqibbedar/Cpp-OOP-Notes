Keyword: These are predefined words that are reserved by the programming language because they have special meaning. Each keyword has specific function and you cannot use them as variables name. For example: int, float, if, else, for, while etc are keywords. (language defined words)

Identifier: These are the names given by the programmers to entities such as variables, functions, array etc. They are user-defined and they hep in identifying the entities in a program. For example, int a = 10; "a" is an identifier.
